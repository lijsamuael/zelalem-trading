Who We Are
ZELELEM KEBEDE EXPORT IMPORT is the company established in 2010 under Ethiopian Commercial law and engaged in import of vehicles, and other goods for Ethiopian market. The company has been an international supplier of China top brands construction equipment for more than 10 years! A privately owned company, it has established itself as a Russian, Eastern Europe and Middle Asia markets leader and one of the largest specialists in vehicles and equipment sales.
